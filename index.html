About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Astrophysics at the University of Victoria, supervised by Dr. Wesley Fraser. My research focuses on exploring the Trans-Neptunian region using the James Webb Space Telescope (JWST). I am a member of several international collaborations, including the Vera C. Rubin Observatory LSST Solar System Science Collaboration (LSST-SSSC), the CLASSY survey, and the CASTOR mission. My work aims to uncover the nature and origins of small, faint bodies in the outer Solar System.

Research Projects
